Hi, Welcome to BLang Yesho!

BLang is an Accounting Application for :

  • Recording day to day Transactions of your Business in the Books of Accounts.
  • Classifying such Transactions into individual Ledgers.
  • Generating Financial Statements to evaluate Performance and Position of Businesses.
  • Helps in calculation and filing of Taxation at your Businesses.
  • Automated Accounting Application, BLang makes Books of Accounts effective.
